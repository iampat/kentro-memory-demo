## Ticket #142 - Acme Corp

**Status:** Open
**Severity:** Medium
**Reported by:** Sandra Kowalski, Operations Manager
**Date:** 2026-04-19
**Subject:** Dashboard loading latency during peak business hours

---

### Description

Since approximately April 17th, several Acme Corp users have reported significant delays when loading the main analytics dashboard. Page load times have increased from the usual 2–3 seconds to upwards of 18–25 seconds, primarily between 9:00 AM and 11:30 AM EST. The issue appears consistent across Chrome and Edge browsers and affects at least 14 confirmed user accounts.

Affected users are able to log in without issue, and other areas of the platform (reports, settings, billing) load normally. Sandra has confirmed that no internal network changes were made on their end prior to the onset of the issue. A screen recording and HAR file have been attached for review.
